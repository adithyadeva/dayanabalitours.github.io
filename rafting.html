Ayung River Bali as a rafting tourist location is considered the best white water rafting in Bali. Because this white water river is perfect for beginners and very suitable for family activities. The length of the Ayung River is approximately 68.5 kilometers. Starting from the northern mountain, then heading to Bangli Regency, Gianyar Regency, Badung Regency, and ending at the Badung Strait on the Sanur coast of Bali. Ayung river rafting is the first white water river used for Bali rafting trips. As one of the longest rivers in Bali, Dayana Bali rafting adventure offers a thrilling adventure combined with nature tourism in central Bali.

If you do Rafting Bali, then you will enjoy the Bali rain forest and take care of the rice fields. You may also be able to spot wildlife in Bali's rainforests such as the beautiful Blue Java Kingfisher. If you want to find a hidden waterfall, then you can find it by rafting at Dayana. The scenery and wildlife that you can get here such as rice fields, forests, and waterfalls.
